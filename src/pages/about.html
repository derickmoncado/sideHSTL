About

The Story

I'm Derick, the guy behind sideHSTL, I'm a versatile freelance creative based in California's Capital City and have been practicing design and web development collectively for around 10 years.

Originally, I wanted to be a filmmaker. But the cost of film school and 2008's recession just didn't allow that to come to fruition. So I shifted gears and enrolled in a Design program here in Sacramento.

After college, I initially focused primarily just in design, but after seeing 2010's The Social Network in theaters with some friends, I got inspired to persue web development and taught myself how to code.

I've been designing and building ever since.

A designer who codes...

..a Coder who designs.. and shoots. It really just depends what type of project I'm persuing, or the type of project that lands in my lap, either way I love solving complex problems in a creative and personal way that's on brand.

In the past I've worked at small startups, to full-service agencies that have had an impressive roster of clients.

I've tackled projects ranging from quick weekend jobs, to full fledge web applications who's development phase spanned several months.

I've worked with clients all accross the US and even with some of the big boys over in the Bay Area. I'm truly greatful for some of the opportunities that have come my way and the opportunites yet to introduce themselves.
